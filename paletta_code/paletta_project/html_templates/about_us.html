Welcome to Paletta

Store. Search. Share.

Smarter Access to Academic Footage

Purpose-Built for Education

Paletta is a premium video library designed specifically for educational content. We source authentic footage directly from top universities and colleges to ensure your projects reflect real academic environments.

Authenticity Over Generic Stock

We go beyond standard stock footage. Our clips capture the true spirit of academic life and subject-specific visuals that generic libraries simply can't offer.

Curated for Creators

Whether you're an educator, marketer, or content creator, our library gives you access to high-quality, relevant footage tailored to your needs — by subject, location, or style.

Visuals with Impact

Every clip is selected for its clarity, accuracy, and storytelling power — helping your work connect with purpose, professionalism, and originality.

Our Team

Niklaas van Poortvliet

Founder & CEO

Niklaas is the founder of Paletta and CEO of Filmbright, a creative agency partnering with leading universities to bring academic stories to life. With over a decade of experience in educational media and content strategy, he created Paletta to help institutions store, manage, and share their most valuable visual assets — from campus cutaways to high-end research footage.

Based in Bavaria, Niklaas leads on product vision, strategy, and partnerships — blending storytelling, design, and technology to make academic footage more discoverable and impactful.
